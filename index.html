Ingredients :

  • Ice cubes
  • Rasberries
  • Kwis
  • Pop mould

Recipe:

  1. Blend the fresh fruits separately in a blender.
  2. Add some water to it to get a right consistency.
  3. Next add the flavored liquid to the pop molds.
  4. Freeze it for hours.
  5. Then enjoy the perfect homemade yummy ice pops !!!!!
